❓ Frequently Asked Cross-Border Sync Questions

How do I coordinate a meeting with team members in London, Colombo, and New York?

Coordinating between London (GMT), Colombo (IST), and New York (EST) requires identifying a narrow intersection point. When New York starts its business morning at 9:00 AM EST, London is at 2:00 PM GMT, and Colombo is at 7:30 PM IST. TimeCompass lets you quickly lock this window.

What are standard global business working hours?

Standard global business parameters typically map between 8:00 AM and 6:00 PM local time. This tool establishes strict tracking constraints centered around these optimal windows.
